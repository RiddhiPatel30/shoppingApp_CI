How Can We Help You?

If you have any questions or need assistance with our app, you’re in the right place! Below are some common questions and troubleshooting tips:

How to Create an Account

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the top right corner of the page. You will be prompted to enter your email, choose a password, and fill in your personal details. Once you're done, click "Submit" to complete the registration process.

How to Add Products to Your Cart

To add products to your shopping cart, simply browse through our product categories or search for items you want. Once you find a product, click the "Add to Cart" button. Your selected items will appear in the cart at the top right corner of the page.

How to Checkout

After adding items to your cart, click the cart icon to review your order. Once you’re ready to purchase, click the "Checkout" button. You will be prompted to enter your shipping details and payment information. Once confirmed, your order will be processed, and you will receive a confirmation email.

Order Tracking

You can track your order by visiting the "Order History" section in your account. Once your order has shipped, you will receive tracking details via email.

Payment Methods Accepted

We accept a variety of payment methods, including major credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and other secure online payment systems.

Still Need Help?

If you couldn't find an answer to your question, feel free to reach out to our support team. You can contact us via the Contact Us page.
